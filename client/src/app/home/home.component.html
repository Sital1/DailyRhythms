What is DailyRhythm?

DailyRhythm helps you organize your day into manageable tasks categorized into Morning, Afternoon, Evening, and Anytime. It's designed to enhance your productivity by keeping track of your daily activities and helping you focus on what's important.

How to Use DailyRhythms

Getting started with DailyRhythms is easy:

  1. Sign up and create your account.
  2. Add tasks to your ToDo list and categorize them based on when you plan to do them.
  3. Track your progress daily and update tasks as you complete them.
  4. Review your daily logs to see your productivity patterns and improve.
